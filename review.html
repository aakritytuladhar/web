wrote a review Dec 2019


I spent three weeks in Nepal on my own and I decided to challenge myself and to try to reach the Everest Best Camp. I went to Thamel Travels and Tours agency right in the centre of Thamel and Balaram, the owner, was very helpful and eager to answer any of my doubts (and I had many). He arranged everything, he borrowed me a very good sleeping bed and a bottom jacket, and he picked me up at my hostel to leave to Lukla the following day. My guide, Niraj, was amazing: he has always been available to solve any problem I had and he proved to be an excellent guide for everything, always making me feel comfortable also in the middle of Himalayas. The price was really reasonable.
Overall, I would definitely recommend this agency, and I would choose it again if I am ever going to Nepal again.
